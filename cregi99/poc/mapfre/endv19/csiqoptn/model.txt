%DDNAME=LOADSCL,COND=SUCCESS
  LOAD MEMBER &C1MEMBER
    FROM DSNAME '&C1FDSN'
    TO  ENVIRONMENT  &ENV
        SYSTEM       &SYS
        SUBSYSTEM    &SBS
        TYPE         &C1TYPE
    OPTIONS CCID '&CCID'
        COMMENT '&COMMENT'
        PROCESSOR GROUP &C1PRGRP
    FOOTPRINT '&C1UDSN'
  .
