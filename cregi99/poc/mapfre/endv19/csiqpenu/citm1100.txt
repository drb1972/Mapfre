+The ENVIRONMENT SELECTION LIST appears when you first access
 Endevor from an ISPF/PDF PRIMARY OPTIONS MENU, if:

    - More than one environment is defined at your site.

       AND

    - Your TSO userid is authorized to use more than one.

 A list of all environments available within the C1DEFLTS Table is dis-
 played.  Each environment is identified by a 1-8 character name,
 followed by a brief description.

 Select the environment you want to use by typing the appropriate option
 number in the%option+field at the top of the panel.
