%Remote Job Model Member Root+- The root portion of a member named
+   #RJrootx, where "root" is the character string specified in this
+   field and "x" is a suffix.  Such a set of members are used to
+   generate a JCL stream to be executed at the remote site after
+   the shipment staging data sets have been transmitted.  The members
+   are stored in iprfx.iqual.CSIQOPTN
+
+   If this field is left blank, the value specified in ENDICNFG will
+   be used.  The field can be from 1 to 4 characters long.  Valid
+   characters are A-Z, 0-9, $, #, and @. Imbedded blanks are not
+   allowed.
+
+   The roots of remote JCL model members distributed with the product are:
+
+   BLUE - IEBCOPY and IDCAMS are used to copy/delete members into
+          remote production datasets.
+
+   FAST - FASTCOPY is used to copy/delete members
